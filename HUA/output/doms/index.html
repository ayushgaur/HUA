Employee Speak

Good company to work for. I express my gratitude to Mukesh for hiring me and giving me a change to excel.

28 July 2010, I can never forget this date. The day when I entered in a different phase of life i.e. Professional World. So, I'll be completing 5 years in this organization which gives me a sense of belonging to QA Info Tech. I never thought that this journey would be so exciting and challenging. Each and every day was a different learning experience here. Senior Management, colleagues, everyone is so helpful and nice. I feel like I am totally a different person now. I entered this organization, as a young nervous boy and I am a Young Confident Man today. I've got a lot of exposure here that had helped me to enhance my knowledge and intellect. The best part of the organization is that there are enormous opportunities and management will always help you to grab them and work on them. They will always be there to provide their continuous support and will always show interest in helping you to learn and do the task in a better way. As it is said that," All time Work and No Play... Makes Jack a dull Boy". So, our Organization takes care of this aspect also. Fun events are there on regular basis that keeps us fresh and refreshes our mind. It's always like a second home to me. There were lots of ups and downs but we stood as a team and faced it and we have always seen it growing better and better and so was our personal front. So far, it has been a wonderful and awesome time here and still my journey is on... Lots of new learning, lot of new exposures, lot of new projects are yet to come. Each day I enter my office to do something new or if not new than do the regular job in a new way which keeps my mind innovative. And more stories are yet to come. As i said, "THE JOURNEY IS STILL ON"...
